SMAK STELLA MARIS SURABAYA 2023/2023

Studentship

Student activities as activities to develop students'

interests and talents while studying at SMAK Stella Maris. Student activities at SMAK Stella Maris include:

A. Extracurricular

Extracurricular activities at SMAK Stella Maris are divided into 2 (two) types, namely compulsory extracurricular and elective extracurricular.

There are 3 (three) extracurriculars, namely: Scouts, Gamelan (for male students)

and Traditional Dance (for female students). While there are 10 extracurricular options namely:

Basketball, Futsal, Volleyball, Badminton, Journalism, Modern Dance, Choir (Stelma Choir), Bridge, E-Sport, and Band.

B. STELMA OSIS

C. LETS Character Education L(Loving), E (Excellent), T (Transformation), S (Solidarity)

Sr. Lucia Dipoyudo, SPM (late) from the Santa Perawan Maria Foundation is the founder of Stella Maris High school
