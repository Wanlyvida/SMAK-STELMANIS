NEW STUDENT REGISTRATION REQUIREMENTS:

1. Take/buy the Registration Form Rp. 175.000,-

2. Submit the Registration Form which has been accompanied by the following required files:

➢ 1 copy of grade 7 & 8 report cards (odd and even semesters)

➢ 1 copy of birth certificate & family card (KSK)

➢ 1 copy of Baptism Certificate (for Catholics)

➢ 2 recent passport size photos 3 x 4

➢ Photocopy of the championship award certificate obtained in junior high school (for those who have)

➢ Copy of SKTM, Smart Indonesia Card (for those who have)
